## Implementation note

Group bonuses such as `S+10` mean the bonus applies once if any selected ingredient belongs to that group. Before converting this seed to JSON, audit duplicate mechanical identities (same requirements and near-identical modifiers), reachability with exactly three slots, semantic naming, and balance. Minimal corrections are allowed but must be documented in the implementation handoff.
